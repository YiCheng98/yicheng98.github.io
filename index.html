I am a third-year PhD student researching Natural Language Processing (NLP) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), advised by Prof. Maggie Wenjie Li. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in computer science at Peking University, where I was honored to work with Prof. Sujian Li. I have interned at Tencent Jarvis Research Center since 2016, where I worked with Dr. Yefeng Zheng and Prof. Bang liu.

Research

My broad research interest NLP for social good. In the recent years, my research has specifically focused on the following directions:

  • NLP applications for mental healthcare (e.g. emotional support conversation systems, empathetic chatbot).
  • NLP applications in the medical field, especially medical consultation dialogue systems.
